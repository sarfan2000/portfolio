Service Center Management System

Project Overview

A complete management platform built for automobile service centers to digitize and streamline their day-to-day operations. The system replaces manual paper-based workflows with an efficient digital solution covering the entire service lifecycle — from customer walk-in to final billing.

Objectives

  • Automate customer registration and vehicle intake process
  • Enable real-time service job tracking and status updates
  • Manage appointment scheduling to reduce wait times
  • Generate accurate billing and invoicing automatically
  • Improve overall operational efficiency through digital workflows

Key Features

Customer Registration Automated customer profiles and vehicle records
Appointment Scheduling Online booking and slot management system
Service Tracking Real-time job status and technician assignment
Billing & Invoicing Automated cost calculation and invoice generation
Vehicle Management Complete vehicle history and service records
Reports & Analytics Operational reports and performance insights

System Architecture

  • Frontend: React.js with component-based UI for a responsive dashboard
  • Backend: Node.js + Express.js RESTful API handling all business logic
  • Database: MongoDB for flexible document storage of customer and vehicle data
  • Authentication: JWT-based secure login for staff and admin roles

Technologies Used

React.js Node.js Express.js MongoDB JWT REST API
